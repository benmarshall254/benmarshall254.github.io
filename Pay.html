How to Pay for VIP Predictions

Choose the payment method that works best for you to get access to our premium daily tips.

1. Pay via M-Pesa

  1. Go to your M-Pesa menu on your phone.
  2. Select Send Money or Pay Bill.
  3. Enter the Business Number: 123456 (replace with your actual till number).
  4. Enter Account Number: VIPTips (or your account ID).
  5. Enter the amount you want to pay (e.g., Ksh 500).
  6. Enter your M-Pesa PIN and confirm the transaction.
  7. Send us a confirmation message with your phone number to +254 722 542 685 to activate your VIP access.

2. Pay via PayPal

  1. Click the PayPal button below to pay securely.
  2. Enter the amount and complete the payment.
  3. Send us your PayPal transaction ID via WhatsApp or email to activate your VIP access.
